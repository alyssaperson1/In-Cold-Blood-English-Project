Our Developers and Authors

We are a team of writers and a web designer with a dream; a dream to get an A on our project. Our project is to develop an argument for the novel, In Cold Blood; whether Dick and Perry should be sentenced to a death penalty or a life sentence. For more information, visit our thesis and evidence page. A big shoutout to website designer Alyssa, and authors Josie and Caitlin.

Website Goal

Our idea for this website is to explore the death penalty punishment, and the ethics of it all. We act as in the perspective of the jury to convey our message: whether Dick and Perry (who had killed and robbed the Clutters family) deserve a life sentence or a death punishment. Now, it is known that they were hung in the state of Kansas, but if you visit our historical context page, you will find that Kansas actually had a history of hanging people as punishment.

How to Use this Website

How you use this website is below, you will find that there are a selection of buttons. Click any, and it will lead you to your desired location for information. All of the titles should correctly line up with the corresponding point on the rubric and task list for the argument.
